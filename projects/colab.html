COLAB

Web-app platform for students

COLAB is a web-app platform for students to collaborate on projects and share ideas. The platform is designed to be easy to use and intuitive, with a focus on user experience and accessibility. COLAB is built using NextJS and Firebase, and features a clean and modern design that is optimized for both desktop and mobile devices. The platform is currently in development, with new features and improvements being added regularly.
